1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use Speakary: The 4-Minute Journal ("the App," or "we"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to handling your personal information transparently and responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information from users:

  • Apple User ID (via Sign in with Apple)
  • Profile Picture (uploaded and stored in Firebase)
  • Voice Recordings
  • Mood/Moment type selections
  • Anonymous analytics and device information (e.g. app usage, crash logs)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide core features, including cloud backups
  • Authenticate users via Sign in with Apple
  • Improve the app experience through aggregated analytics
  • Send essential app notifications and occasional promotional messages

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Authentication, Firestore, and Firebase Storage. All data is encrypted in transit and protected by properly configured Firebase Security Rules. Only the authenticated user has access to their own data. No public or unauthorized access is permitted.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Delete your account and all data from within the app
  • Update your profile picture, username, or birthday at any time
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

6. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.
